# References: Capstone Projects and Continuous Practice

1. [Capstone course](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capstone_course) - Wikipedia - Background on integrative culminating projects in professional education; frames the pedagogical purpose of the capstone variants offered in this chapter.

2. [Continuous improvement process](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Continual_improvement_process) - Wikipedia - Coverage of Kaizen, PDCA, and other continuous-improvement frameworks that this chapter applies to ongoing token-cost optimization.

3. [DevOps](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DevOps) - Wikipedia - The cultural and tooling movement behind continuous delivery; informs the continuous-cost-monitoring posture this chapter recommends.

4. AI Engineering - Chip Huyen - O'Reilly - The capstone-style end-to-end deployment and monitoring chapters parallel the integrative scope of this textbook's capstone projects.

5. Accelerate: The Science of Lean Software and DevOps - Nicole Forsgren, Jez Humble, Gene Kim - IT Revolution - The empirical research behind continuous-improvement practices and the four key metrics; useful framing for the engineering-management deliverables in the capstone.

6. [Google SRE Workbook](https://sre.google/workbook/table-of-contents/) - Google - The companion to the SRE book with practical chapters on SLO design, error budgets, and postmortem culture that map directly to the cost-budget framing of the capstone.

7. [The Twelve-Factor App](https://12factor.net/) - Adam Wiggins - The classic checklist for building cloud-native applications; the logging and configuration factors directly inform the capstone's instrumentation design.

8. [Anthropic: Building Effective Agents](https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/building-effective-agents) - Anthropic Engineering - The reference essay on production agent design including budget and observability recommendations applicable to the autonomous-coding-harness capstone variant.

9. [LangSmith Documentation](https://docs.smith.langchain.com/) - LangChain - Reference for the production-observability platform that supports the cost-attribution and trace-analysis deliverables of the capstone project.

10. [LangFuse Documentation](https://langfuse.com/docs) - LangFuse - Open-source alternative to LangSmith for LLM observability and cost tracking; relevant for engineers whose capstone runs in a self-hosted environment.
